What is it?

My Jacobs is a student-developped platform intended for Jacobs University student to help them through their journey. It provides multiple functionalities and information that is mostly needed by Jacobs community.

Functionalities

MY ACADEMICS

Grouped by semester, the application will display in a tabular manner registered and previous classes, current grades, and GPAs. Additionally, for classes that has an assignment part of a project part, respective grades will be pulled from Moodle and displayed to students in the same table. Also, a count of the total/average of these grades will be displayed. A Colours Code will be used inside the table to differentiate between class types (core, choice, skills …). Also, a special label will be assigned to classes that have assignment prerequisite.

MY FACILITIES

Based on each student address inside campus, the availability of laundry room of the specific college and block will be present (available or full), and availability of kitchen of specific college, block and floor will also be displayed. If time permit, status of the adjacent facilities will also be displayed (adjacent floors for kitchen and adjacent blocks for laundry rooms). In this same field, if a facility if available students may change the status (mark as full) if they want to use the facility. The update of the facility status will be available to all.

CALLS

Will display all current available calls in a tabular manner. Any student is free to accept a “call”. Upon acceptance, the student that mad the “call” shall receive a notification and the current call shall get deleted from the list. Students will be able to submit a call. A call will contain an id, a title, a description, a post and expiration dates and a reward. Upon a complete submission of a call, students may find the respective call in “calls” section
